About me

Qiuyang Lin received the B.S. degree in electrical engineering from Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China, in 2014, and the M.S. degree in electrical engineering from Delft University of Technology, Delft, The Netherlands, in 2016, respectively.

He then joined IMEC, Leuven, Belgium, and Katholieke Universiteit Leuven, Belgium, where he is currently working towrad a Ph.D. degree in biomedical integrated circuit design.

His research interests include biomedical readout amplifiers and data converters, low-power, low-noise, mixed-signal circuits and systems for optical sensors and other wearable biomedical applications.
